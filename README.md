## Overview

MotionScape is a real-world UAV video dataset designed for world model training and evaluation in highly dynamic aerial scenarios. The dataset contains diverse scenes, weather conditions, and free 6-DoF camera motions, and is intended to support research on video generation, spatial-temporal modeling, and controllable aerial scene understanding.
